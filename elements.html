Get Scouted

A Quick Guide

If you’re contacted by steelcitymodels, we’ll guide you through the next steps so you know exactly what to expect. There’s no pressure — just a chance to explore modelling and see if it’s something you’d enjoy. Being scouted is just the starting point, and we’re here to help you take it from there.

You can also apply directly by filling in the form below. It’s a quick way to send your details to the steelcitymodels team, and it helps us get a better idea of who you are and what you’re interested in. Once you submit the application, someone from steelcitymodels will review it and get back to you if we think you’re a good fit
